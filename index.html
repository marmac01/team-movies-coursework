Doctor Sleep (2018) - Rated 15

Mike Flanagan's adaptation of the sequel of Stephen King's The Shining.

Danny (Played by Ewan McGregor) is still recovering from what happened back at the Overlook hotel. His young friend Abra (Played by Kyliegh Curran) is targeted by an evil cult let by Rose the Hat(Rebecca Ferguson). Will Danny be able to save Abra from the evil cult?

Beetlejuice! - Rated 15

Viewing of Tim Burton's Beetlejuice, hosted by us!

Come along to Kaim cottage on the 24th October to watch Tim Burton's award winning Beetlejuice!
This Fantasy-comedy-horror revolves around a recently deceased couple (Played by Alec Baldwin and Geena Davis) and an obnoxious poltergeist named Betelgeuse (Played by Michael Keaton).

Zombieland: Double Tap (2018) - Rated 15

The well received sequel to the hit comedy Zombieland.

Woody Harrelson, Jesse Eisenberg, Abigail Breslin and Emma stone return as Tallahassee, Columbus, Little Rock and Wichita in this action packed, fun filled sequel.
10 years after the first film, we follow our returning cast as they fight off the zombie masses in an effort to survive the post apocalypse.

Halloween (2018) - Rated 18

The long awaited reboot of the iconic Halloween franchise.

Michael has escaped from the institution that held him for so long, and returns to Haddonfield on halloween night.
Will Laurie Strode (played by Jamie Lee Curtis) be able to stop the shape as he massacres his way through halloween night?
