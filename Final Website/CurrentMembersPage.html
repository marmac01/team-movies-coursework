Our Current President

A message from our current president

Welcome to the Film Society, a society of film geeks just wanting to share our passion for the medium.
Every week we have a meetup to discuss the latest films and to allow for members within the society the socialise with like-minded individuals.
We hope that you will enjoy partaking in conversation with other students with the same interests as you and that you will enjoy attending any of our movie screenings.
If you have any questions or concerns you can contact me through my email, the contact us page, or in person at any of our meetups.
